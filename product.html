How SimpleGrid Works — custom ERP, live in 7 days.

Schema-driven, AI-native ERP for manufacturers. No modules, no consultants, no 18-month rollout. Deploy in 7 days. Walk away if it doesn't work in 30.

SimpleGrid does not ship pre-built modules. Instead, our AI writes a complete operational blueprint (SG Schema) of YOUR factory in a 2-3 hour founder conversation. The SG Engine reads the schema and generates the working system: forms, workflows, validations, dashboards, role permissions. Every change after go-live is a row in a table, not a 4-week consultant engagement.
