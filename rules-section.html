Your process, enforced

Every factory has rules that only the people inside it understand.

Approval chains. Vendor exceptions. QC gates that differ per buyer. These rules live in your head, in messaging apps, in notebooks. In our system, they live in configuration, and the system enforces them automatically.

Your approval chain has exceptions

"POs over $10K need founder sign-off, but 3 trusted vendors get a higher limit"

→

Both rules enforced automatically. No multi-week change order, no consultant fees.

Your QC works differently per buyer

"Retailer A needs 75% shelf life, Retailer B accepts 60%"

→

One rule per buyer. System blocks dispatch automatically if the product is too old for that buyer.

Your rework goes to the original contractor

"QC fails 40 pieces; original contractor redoes it at no cost"

→

Rework auto-routes to the same contractor. Rate = $0. Done.

Your shipments have size limits

"Buyer's dock handles 40 CBM, order is 55 CBM"

→

System splits into two shipments, generates two packing lists, updates the order. No manual work.
