Reorder Point + EOQ Calculator - never run out, never tie up cash.

Reorder point = (average daily usage x lead time in days) + safety stock. Order at this level and you never run out or tie up cash in excess stock. Enter usage and lead time to get your reorder point and economic order quantity (EOQ).

Most mid-market manufacturers either order too late (stockouts) or too early (excess cash tied up). Reorder Point + Economic Order Quantity fix both. This calculator uses APICS-standard formulas with safety stock calibrated to your chosen service level.
