What separates a winning RFQ response from a losing one

Format matters more than most manufacturers think. A plain email response with a price and a delivery date competes against your competitor\'s branded PDF with confirmed specs, tiered pricing, terms, and a signature block. Same price, different perception. Buyers are humans - they trust professional presentation, even on commodity items.

The five things every RFQ response should include

  1. Reference the RFQ number and date. Shows you read what they sent.
  2. Quote validity period. Standard is 30 days. Material-volatile items: 7-14 days with escalation clause.
  3. Tiered pricing on multi-quantity items. Gives the buyer choices and makes you look planned.
  4. Lead time per line item. Different parts have different lead times - say so.
  5. Terms in writing. Payment, FOB, certifications. Eliminates surprises later.

Frequently asked questions

What format should an RFQ response be in?

PDF, always. Email body for the cover note ("Please find quotation attached") plus a structured PDF attachment with line items, prices, lead times, and terms.

How long should a quote be valid?

Standard manufacturing practice is 30 days. For volatile material (steel, copper, plastics in supply-constrained periods), drop to 7-14 days and add an escalation clause: "prices subject to material cost adjustment beyond validity."

Should I include terms in the quote?

Yes. Payment terms, FOB point, lead time, quality requirements, certification availability. Putting them in writing prevents arguments later and signals professionalism upfront.

When should I offer tiered pricing?

On parts where unit cost drops meaningfully at higher quantities (setup-heavy items, items with material bulk discounts). Two tiers minimum: "as quoted" and "at 5x volume." Sometimes three.

How quickly should I respond to an RFQ?

Same day if possible; 2-3 business days at the absolute outside. Speed-to-quote is a top-3 factor in RFQ win rate (MAPI). If the work needs more engineering time, send an acknowledgment within hours and a firm quote within a week.
