How to use this invoice generator

  1. Type your company info once — it saves for next time.
  2. Add the customer. Save them to your library so you don't retype.
  3. Add line items. Set tax, shipping, discount.
  4. Click Download — PDF lands in your Downloads folder, branded and ready to email.

What to include on a manufacturing invoice

  • Invoice number (sequential) and issue date.
  • Due date + payment terms (Net 30 is the manufacturing default).
  • Your customer's PO number — they need it to pay you.
  • Line items with SKU, description, qty, unit price.
  • Sales tax if you collect it; shipping if you charged for it.
  • Payment instructions — ACH, wire, check. ACH is the cheapest for manufacturing-sized invoices.
  • Late fee notice if you charge one (1.5%/month is industry standard).

FAQ

Is this invoice generator free?

Yes. Browser-based, no signup, no email wall, unlimited invoices.

Where does my data go?

Nowhere — runs entirely in your browser. PDF is built locally and downloaded to your device. Customer library and recent invoices save to your browser's localStorage.

Does it calculate sales tax automatically?

No — you enter the tax rate. Sales tax in the US varies by state, county, and sometimes city, and exemptions matter for B2B sales. We don't want to give you the wrong number.

Can I use this for international customers?

The PDF is in USD only. For international invoicing in other currencies or with VAT, you'll outgrow this fast.
