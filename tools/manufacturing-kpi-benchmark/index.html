The 5 KPIs that tell you everything

Manufacturers track dozens of metrics. These 5 capture the operation: on-time delivery (customer-facing), scrap rate (quality + cost), inventory turns (cash efficiency), gross margin (financial health), revenue per employee (operational productivity). Score against industry benchmarks and you know where you stand in 3 minutes.

Benchmark sources

  • APICS/ASCM SCOR model - on-time delivery, inventory turns
  • IndustryWeek Best Plants - scrap rate
  • US Census Bureau Annual Survey of Manufactures - gross margin, revenue per employee
  • Aberdeen Group - cross-cutting mid-market manufacturing data
