Customer Concentration Risk - the silent valuation killer.

Customer concentration = largest customer revenue / total revenue. Above 20 to 30 percent, buyers and lenders discount your business. Enter your top customers to see your risk and its effect on valuation.

Top customer over 25% of revenue typically costs 15-25% in valuation discount (HBR). This calculator scores your concentration via the Herfindahl-Hirschman Index, models loss scenarios, and quantifies the valuation hit.
