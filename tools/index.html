Why we built these

We talk to mid-market US manufacturers every week - 50 to 1,500 employees, no IT team, founder-led. Almost every one of them does the same paperwork the same way: a PO template in Word, an invoice in QuickBooks, a quote in Excel, a job-cost spreadsheet someone keeps "fixing", a production board on a whiteboard, a QC checklist on a clipboard.

That works. Until it doesn't. The wall comes around 50 open jobs, or the third time you lose a PO between the floor and the office, or the first time you realize the 600-piece batch was actually unprofitable because nobody added up the rework.

These tools give you the polished version of each piece of paper for free. We hope you use them. If you eventually find yourself juggling six of them at once and wishing the data flowed between them - that's the moment to talk to us.

What's in each tool

  • Purchase Order Generator - buyer + vendor info, line items with tax and shipping, PDF download, vendor library saved locally to your browser.
  • Invoice Generator - customer billing details, payment terms, auto due-date calc, PDF download.
  • Quote / Estimate Generator - same fields as invoice plus a "valid until" date and an acceptance signature line.
  • Job Cost Calculator - materials + direct labor + machine time + subcontractor + overhead. Compares against your target selling price to show margin.
  • Bill of Materials Builder - parent SKU with multiple component rows, per-unit qty and cost, computes assembly cost. CSV + PDF export.
  • Production Schedule - list your jobs with start/end dates and status. Visual Gantt for the floor.
  • Quality Inspection Checklist - checklist items with criteria, actual readings, and pass/fail. Signed PDF report.
  • Markup vs Margin Calculator - three modes: cost-to-price, price-to-margin check, side-by-side comparison table. The pricing error that costs manufacturers 5-15%.
  • Revenue per Employee Benchmark - compare your operational efficiency to US Census Bureau manufacturing benchmarks for your vertical. Median + top quartile.
  • Scrap & Waste Cost Calculator - calculate the full cost of scrap (material + labor + overhead + lost revenue) and see what dropping the rate by half is worth.
  • Do I Need an ERP? Diagnostic - 8 questions, 3 minutes. Quantifies the annual cost of operating without a system.

How private are these tools?

Very. Everything runs in your browser. The numbers you type never leave your device unless you decide to share the PDF. We don't have a server-side database for any of this - there's no signup, no account, no cloud sync. Your vendor list and recent documents save to your browser's localStorage only.
