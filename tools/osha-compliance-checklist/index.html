OSHA Compliance Self-Audit - 25 essentials, real penalty exposure.

Average OSHA serious violation: $15,625. Average workplace injury: $42K direct + $126K indirect. This checklist covers the 25 most-cited manufacturing items so you can find gaps before an inspector does.

25 essential OSHA items for manufacturers. Based on OSHA self-inspection checklists + 29 CFR 1910. Average serious violation penalty: $15,625 (2024). Average workplace injury: $42,000 direct + $126,000 indirect (NSC).

Item 1 of 25 - Critical
Aisles + passageways clear, no obstructions
Item 2 of 25 - Critical
Floor surfaces dry, free from oil/water
Item 3 of 25 - Critical
Elevated platforms have guardrails (42" top, 21" mid)
Item 4 of 25 - Critical
Written Emergency Action Plan posted (>10 employees)
Item 5 of 25 - Critical
Fire extinguishers mounted within 75 ft, inspected monthly
Item 6 of 25 - Critical
Emergency exits open outward, not blocked
Item 7 of 25 - Critical
Electrical panels have 36" clearance, no blocked
Item 8 of 25 - Critical
No exposed wiring, no frayed cords
Item 9 of 25 - Critical
Machine guards on all point-of-operation hazards
Item 10 of 25 - Critical
Power transmission fully enclosed (belts, pulleys)
Item 11 of 25 - Critical
E-stops accessible at each machine, tested monthly
Item 12 of 25 - Critical
PPE Hazard Assessment documented
Item 13 of 25 - Critical
Eye protection where flying particles exist
Item 14 of 25 - Critical
Written HazCom program maintained
Item 15 of 25 - Critical
SDS for all hazardous chemicals, accessible
Item 16 of 25 - Critical
All containers labeled (incl. secondary)
Item 17 of 25 - Critical
Written LOTO procedures per equipment type
Item 18 of 25 - Critical
Each authorized employee has personal lock
Item 19 of 25 - Critical
Annual LOTO procedure inspection documented
Item 20 of 25 - Important
Noise survey done (areas over 85 dBA TWA)
Item 21 of 25 - Critical
Hearing Conservation Program for over-85 dBA exposures
Item 22 of 25 - Critical
Forklift operators trained + certified (3-yr recert)
Item 23 of 25 - Important
Forklift daily pre-use inspection logged
Item 24 of 25 - Critical
Overhead cranes inspected annually
Item 25 of 25 - Important
Capacity posted on cranes/hoists
