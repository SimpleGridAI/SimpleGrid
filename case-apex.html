Apex Apparel - custom ERP, live in 12 days.

An 80–100k shirts/month apparel manufacturer. Three independent business streams. 30+ inventory locations. 20+ job workers. We modeled the operation as it actually runs - and shipped working software in 12 days. They paid us nothing during the build.

Ephemeral SKUs generated per work order. Fabric and trim issuance per cutting ticket. LR-tracked logistics events. Size-wise breakdowns per buyer. Brand-data isolation across streams. None of it fit a generic apparel ERP module. SimpleGrid carried the build at our cost - they ran it 30 days on their real floor before deciding to keep it.
