Built by a manufacturer for manufacturers.

SimpleGrid was built by a founder who ran a $30M manufacturing business, survived two ERP failures, and ended up running operations on Google Sheets. Now we ship the ERP we needed.

The lens on every product decision is "would this work on the floor?" Not "is it technically elegant." Not "does it match the roadmap." Will the warehouse manager in a 400-person furniture factory actually use this? If not, we don't build it. The team includes engineers with shop-floor manufacturing experience — not just software backgrounds.
